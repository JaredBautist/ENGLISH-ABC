# A1.2 English Curriculum - Complete Design Specification

**Level:** A1.2 (CEFR) - Elementary  
**Target Audience:** Adult learners 16+ years old  
**Duration:** 8 weeks (15 slides per week = 120 total slides)  
**Instructional Model:** PPP (Presentation → Practice → Production)  

---

## CURRICULUM OVERVIEW

| Week | Topic | Primary Grammar | Vocabulary Count | Interactive Activities | Cultural Focus |
|------|-------|-----------------|------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Hello! Introductions | To Be (Present) | 32 words | Drag-Drop, Dialogue, Fill-Blank | Greetings Worldwide |
| 2 | Daily Routine | Present Simple ±/± | 15 activities | Timeline, Listening, Description | Daily Schedules |
| 3 | Family | Possessive Adjectives | 20+ words | Family Tree, Match, Description | Family Structures |
| 4 | Home | There is/are + Prep | 23 words | Furniture Placement, Locate, Describe | House Styles |
| 5 | Hobbies &Free Time | Like + -ing + Frequency | 20 hobbies | Survey, Identify, Discussion | Popular Hobbies |
| 6 | Shopping | Have you got? / Can I? | 32 items | Shopping Sim, Dialogue, Role-Play | Market Customs |
| 7 | Food & Eating | Would Like | 30+ foods | Menu Order, Conversation, Create Menu | Food Traditions |
| 8 | Revision & Integration | ALL GRAMMAR | 150+ words | Bingo, Exercises, Listening, Profile | Global Integration |

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

✅ **Pedagogical Alignment:**
- Each week follows PPP model (Presentation → Practice → Production)
- Vocabulary scaffolding (receptive → productive)
- Grammar integrated within communicative contexts

✅ **Interactive Design:**
- Variety of activity types (drag-drop, dialogue, surveys, role-play)
- Immediate feedback with corrective prompts
- Gamification elements (points, progress, achievement)

✅ **Accessibility:**
- 60/40 Visual-to-Text ratio
- IPA pronunciation for ALL vocabulary
- Multiple modalities (listening, reading, speaking, writing)
- Culturally diverse examples

✅ **Assessment:**
- Formative checkpoints per week
- Self-assessment rubrics
- Skills integration (receptive & productive)
