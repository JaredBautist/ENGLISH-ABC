## GRAMMAR OVERVIEW - WEEK 8

### COMPREHENSIVE GRAMMAR REFERENCE TABLE

| Week | Grammar Structure | Form | Example | Use |
|------|-------------------|------|---------|-----|
| 1 | Present "To Be" | I am, You are, He/She is | "I am a teacher." | Identity + intro |
| 2 | Present Simple ±/± | do/does, don't/doesn't | "She works at 9." / "I don't work." | Routines |
| 3 | Possessive Adjectives | my, your, his, her, our, their | "My sister is tall." | Ownership + relation |
| 4 | There is/are | Singular/Plural | "There is a bed. There are 2 chairs." | Location/existence |
| 5 | Like + -ing | Subject + like + -ing | "I like reading." | Interests/preferences |
| 6 | Demonstratives | this, that, these, those | "I want this shirt + that hat." | Pointing to objects |
| 7 | Would Like | I would like (I'd like) + noun | "I would like coffee." | Polite requests |

### ACTIVITY 6: "GRAMMAR PUZZLE 5 - SHOPPING SCENARIO" (Slide 7)

**Activity Type:** Interactive Role-Play  
**Objective:** Practice Demonstratives + Requests  
**Duration:** 3:30 minutes

#### Instructions
1. Role-play shopping in a store
2. Use demonstratives: this, that, these, those
3. Ask for items using "Can I have...?"
4. Complete 6 interactions

#### Shopping Interactions

```
INTERACTION 1:
SHOPKEEPER: "Can I help you?"
YOU: "I want _____ shirt." (this/that/these/those)
→ "I want **this** blue shirt, please."

INTERACTION 2:
SHOPKEEPER: "What size?"
YOU: "I would like _____ in orange." (this/that/these/those - shoes)
→ "I would like **those** in orange, please."

INTERACTION 3:
SHOPKEEPER: "Would you like socks too?"
YOU: "Yes, I would like _____." (this/that/these/those)
→ Yes, I would like **these** black socks."
```

### CAPSTONE STEP 1: "CREATE YOUR CHARACTER" (Slide 10-11)

**Activity Type:** Profile Building + Writing  
**Objective:** Write comprehensive biographical profile  

#### Instructions
1. Create a fictional person (or use yourself)
2. Fill in all sections below
3. Use complete sentences
4. Build 500+ words total

#### PROFILE TEMPLATE

```
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
        INTERNATIONAL PROFILE: [Character Name]
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 1: IDENTITY & BACKGROUND (Week 1)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Name: _______________________________________________
• Age: _______________
• Profession: _______________________________________________
• Home country: _______________________________________________
  
Write 3 sentences introducing yourself:
1. I am [name]. I am [age] years old.
2. I am a [profession].
3. I am from [country].

EXAMPLE:
"I am Maria. I am 28 years old. I am a doctor. I am from Spain."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 2: DAILY ROUTINE (Week 2)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What is your typical weekday?

Write 6-8 sentences about your daily activities using Present Simple:
• When do you wake up? 
• When do you go to work/study?
• What do you do for lunch?
• When do you leave work?
• What do you do in the evening?
• When do you sleep?

EXAMPLE:
"I usually wake up at 7 AM. I eat breakfast at 7:30. I do not have lunch at home; I usually eat at a café near my work. I work until 6 PM. I go home and cook dinner. I sometimes watch TV in the evening. I never sleep late. I always go to bed at 11 PM."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 3: FAMILY (Week 3)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Tell us about your family:

• How many people are in your family?
• Who are they? (Use possessive adjectives: my mother, my brother, etc.)
• Describe them using adjectives

Write 5-7 sentences:

EXAMPLE:
"My family has 5 people. My mother is a teacher. She is kind and friendly. My father is an engineer. He is funny and serious. My sister is 22 years old. She is tall and beautiful. I love my family very much."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 4: HOME (Week 4)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Describe your house/apartment:

• How many rooms are there?
• What furniture is in each room?
• What is your favorite room?

Use "There is" / "There are" + prepositions:

EXAMPLE:
"My apartment has 3 rooms. There is a living room with a big sofa and a TV on the wall. There is a bedroom where my bed is next to the window. There are two lamps in my bedroom. There is a small kitchen with a table. My favorite room is my bedroom because it is quiet and peaceful."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 5: HOBBIES & INTERESTS (Week 5)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What do you like doing in free time?

Use "like + -ing" and frequency adverbs:
• What do you love doing?
• What do you usually do on weekends?
• What do you sometimes do?
• What do you never do?

EXAMPLE:
"I really like reading. I always read books in the evening. I usually like swimming too. I swim every Saturday morning. I sometimes play tennis with friends. I like cooking, and I usually cook on Sundays. I never like watching boring TV shows."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 6: SHOPPING & STYLE (Week 6)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What's your personal style?

• What colors do you like?
• Describe your ideal outfit
• Use demonstratives when discussing preferences

EXAMPLE:
"I like wearing blue and green clothes. I prefer comfortable shirts and pants. I like this style of jacket—it's comfortable and fashionable. I have these shoes that I love. I do not like wearing tight clothes. I prefer simple, practical outfits."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 7: FOOD & DINING (Week 7)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What are your food preferences?

Use "would like" and quantifiers:
• What foods do you like?
• How often do you eat certain foods?
• What's your favorite meal?

EXAMPLE:
"I really like Italian food. For breakfast, I would like coffee with some milk and a few pastries. I usually have pasta for lunch. I like a lot of vegetables with my dinner. I would like chicken and rice three times a week. I would like a little dessert on weekends. I never eat fast food."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SUMMARY: "WHY I'M INTERESTING" (Integrated)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Write 4-5 sentences combining multiple elements:

EXAMPLE:
"I am Maria, a kind doctor from Spain. I have a wonderful family, and my favorite hobby is reading. I love my apartment because there are beautiful views. I usually like spending time with my family, and I would like traveling around the world. I like learning about different cultures and food."

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
