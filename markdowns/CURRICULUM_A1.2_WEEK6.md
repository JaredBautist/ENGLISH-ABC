## GRAMMAR EXPLANATION - WEEK 6

### "CAN I HAVE...?" & "HAVE YOU GOT...?"

#### Rule 1: "CAN I HAVE...?"

Used to politely REQUEST an item.

```
STRUCTURE:
  Can I have + (the/this/that) + [noun]?

EXAMPLES:
  Can I have the blue shirt?
  Can I have this dress?
  Can I have those shoes?

RESPONSES:
  ‚úÖ "Yes, here you are."
  ‚úÖ "Here you go."
  ‚ĚĆ "I'm sorry, we don't have it in red."
```

#### Rule 2: "HAVE YOU GOT...?" (British English)

Similar to "Can I have" but used to CHECK AVAILABILITY.

```
STRUCTURE:
  Have you got + (a/the) + [noun]?
  Or: Do you have...? (American)

EXAMPLES:
  Have you got a blue shirt? (British)
  Do you have a blue shirt? (American)
  Have you got this in red?

RESPONSES:
  Yes, we have.
  No, I'm sorry.
  We have it in black and white.
```

#### Rule 3: DEMONSTRATIVES (this/that/these/those)

```
SINGULAR:
  this  = close (here)
  that  = far (there)

PLURAL:
  these = close (here)
  those = far (there)

USAGE IN SHOPPING:
  ‚úÖ "I like **this** dress." (pointing to close dress)
  ‚úÖ "Can I see **that** one?" (pointing to far item)
  ‚úÖ "I like **these** shoes." (multiple close)
  ‚úÖ "Do you have **those** in red?" (multiple far)
```

#### Example Sentences

1. "**Can I have** the blue shirt?"
2. "**Have you got** this in a smaller size?"
3. "**Do you have** these shoes in black?"
4. "I like **this** dress and **that** one too."
5. "**Can I try on** this jacket?"

### ACTIVITY 3: "INTERACTIVE SHOP ROLE-PLAY" (Slide 8)

**Activity Type:** Interactive Dialogue  
**Objective:** Practice shopping questions and responses  
**Duration:** 3:00 minutes

#### Instructions
1. Act as a customer
2. Shopkeeper greets: "Hello! Can I help you?"
3. You see 4 response options:
   - "Can I have the blue shirt?"
   - "Do you have these in red?"
   - "How much is this?"
   - "Can I try it on?"
